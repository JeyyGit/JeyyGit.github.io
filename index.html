Jeyy Bot

Get an unique discord bot experience!

Jeyy bot is one of the most fun and unique bot on discord. It has many commands such as isometric where you could build your own minecraft-like blocks with 24 available blocks including working redstone parts, gif rendering, saving and sharing builds!


Jeyy Bot consists of unique and fun image generation/manipulation to live up your discord (some of imaging commands are available on Jeyy API), utility commands such as random wheel of fortune, todo list to keep track of your tasks, translator and google search. To make it complete, Jeyy Bot also included external APIs namely reddit, memes, XKCD comics, character guesser, anime expressions, and many more!
